Welcome to Ning's Travel Journal (^_っ^)

I’ve always enjoyed wandering around and exploring new places.

Back in school, I once wrote in my notebook that I dreamed of traveling around the world.

As I grew up, that dream stayed with me, 

but I added a new goal: `To travel the world solo.`

For a long time, this wish just stayed in my mind.

But everything changed after I tried solo travel for the first time in Tokyo.

I completely fell in love with it.

I hope my writing can help fellow travelers who are about to set off on their own journeys.
